TJ Physics Team

Welcome to TJ's Physics Team!

TJHSST's Physics Team has a conducive working environment like no other: we offer engaging lectures, collaborative problem solving sessions, and challenging problem sets. Physics Team is geared towards all students who want to build upon their physics knowledge. We also encourage these dedicated students to participate in competitions such as the National Physics Olympiad and Physics Bowl. In fact, Physics Team has a proud history of producing more members of the US Physics Team than any other high school in the nation. Join us every Friday A Block during 8th period! Since we cater lectures to students' varying abilities, you can be sure to take away new insights every week.

Status
Search hospitals, food banks, and testing centers and click on the marker to see their info. Hospitals, food banks, and testing centers will be able to regularly update their pages to provide information such as items they desperately need and awaiting deliveries and donations. These features allow citizens to address any abundance of supplies they might have, by providing a way that people can see which relief centers have inadequate amounts of supplies and its urgency (denoted by the color coding), so people and organizations can donate to the right facilities. Donations by users and organizations, can be tracked and updated through the status and account sections of our webpage. Current cases are also on the map to give perspective.
Printing
We integrated a system for those with 3D printers to actively contribute to medical facilities who are running short on supplies. Through our account interface, the person in charge of a drop-off or printing facility can upload designs for others to print. Then users can go on the printing page, download the designs, and donate a specific amount. Users can see where the drop-off site is located and when it is open.
